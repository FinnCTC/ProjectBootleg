Mario 7

One of the most iconic bootlegs, Mario 7 (known more commenly as 7 Grand Dad) was released in 1992 for the Mario 7-in-1 game cart for the NES. Mario 7 is a bootleg of, weirdly enough, a Flintstones game.

This bootleg went under the radar until 2014, When youtuber "Vinesauce" discovered the bootleg during a stream. The rest, as they say, is history.

Date: 1992

Publisher: J.Y Company

Console: Famicon
